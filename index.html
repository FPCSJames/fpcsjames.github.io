About Us

Auburn local, serving the entire Southeast - from Dallas to Atlanta and beyond.

Founded in Alabama in 2010, Flashpoint Computer Services has been serving clients in the Auburn-Opelika area, East Texas, and more for over eight years. Its managing director, James M. Joyce, a graduate of Auburn's information systems management program, brings a passion for technology with a keen eye toward business sensibility to every Flashpoint customer - translating the unique needs of each into cost-effective, innovative solutions that last.

Flashpoint has proudly partnered with clients across a diverse range of industries, from finance, to law, heavy industry, retail, and more. FPCS has worked with three four-year accredited colleges and universities, including Auburn University, and has provided expert support to in-house IT departments, other service providers, and sole proprietors alike.
